Session 7

Intro

Hello, young explorers!

Today, our exciting adventure begins in a very special place: the coastal ecosystem. Imagine the waves crashing on the shore, the salty breeze in the air, and the sun warming the sand. This is a home not just for us when we visit the beach, but for countless plants, animals, and even tiny living creatures you can barely see!

But did you know that as visitors and inhabitants of this amazing ecosystem, our actions have a big impact here? Before we dive into our adventure, we’ll learn about how we can take care of this incredible place. Together, we’ll discover what we do that helps or harms the coast and what makes this ecosystem so important for everyone.

Get ready, because today we’re not just visitors—we’re coastal heroes on a mission to protect and explore the wonders of the sea and the shore. Let’s start learning and have fun along the way!

First you will play a Ecosystem race. Roll the dice, advance the squares and answer the questions.

Solutions:

Pictionary + Dice questions Rules

You will play a Pictionary + Dice questions.

One of the group  members will go to the blackboars, the teacher will show him or her a card with a word. That person will have 1 minute to try to represent that word with a drawing, the rest of the group will have to discover the word. If they get it right, that group will get 1 point.

Then, that person will roll a dice, which has 6 different options according to the color:

1. Biodiversity cards - specific questions with specific answers about what we have been learning.

2. Human action cards - questions in the ones you will have to reflect about if an action is good or bad and why.

3.  Create solution cards - questions in the ones you have to create an efficient solution for a specific problem.

4. Another group decides which card you will have to answer to.

5. Your group decides which card to answer to.

6. Your teacher decides which card you have to answer to.

The person that has drawn the previous word has to read the question out loud. The rest of the group has to decide an answer. After they tell the answer, the person with the card will read out loud the solution written in the card. If you get it right, you will get an extra point.

The end!

Congratulations, explorers! You’ve completed your journey through the desert and discovered the amazing ways life thrives in such a harsh environment.

Look at your Explorer's Passport—if you’ve collected all the stamps, it’s time to open the treasure box! Inside, you’ll find a special reward for your hard work and teamwork.

Thank you for joining this adventure and becoming desert experts. Remember, every ecosystem has its own story, and there are so many more to explore.

Until next time, keep learning and keep exploring!
