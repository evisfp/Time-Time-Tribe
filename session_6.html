Session 6

Intro

Good morning, adventurers! Today is a special day because our great adventure begins right here, in our own neighborhood. Look around—every street, every tree, and every corner is part of a bigger story waiting to be discovered.

This is not just any neighborhood. It’s a place full of mysteries, challenges, and hidden treasures. Together, we’ll explore, learn, and uncover the secrets of the world starting right here, where we live.

Are you ready to begin? Let’s step outside, open our eyes wide, and let the adventure unfold! Today, the journey starts at home.

What is in my bag? Instructions

In today´s activity, you are going to work on the Session 1 and 2 vocabulary.

Every travller and guardian needs a good bag. For that reason, every group will have one bag. In that bag you will have to stick the card “I have…”, in the space with the number 1.

Then you have to relate the words of the cards with the images you have, according to the group they belong to (e.g. Word: "descomposer" - image: "fungi"), and stick the ones that you recognize. You will stick those in spaces number 2.

When you finish, you will have to tell the rest groups of the class what is what you have in yout bags. You will have to start every sentence by saying: “I have in my bag a… that is a ....” (e.g.: I have in my bag a fungi that is a descomposer). While you tell the word, you have to point to the image you have stuck in the bag. All of the group members have to speak.

Time to write

Now that we’ve finished the activity, it’s time to write about your experience! In your notebook, write a short paragraph answering these questions:

  1. What did you find in your bag?
  2. How did you decide which group each item belonged to?
  3. What was the most interesting or surprising thing in your bag? Why?
  4. How did you feel when you presented your bag to the class?

Be creative! You can describe how your group worked together, what you learned, and anything funny or challenging that happened. Make sure to use the sentence structure we practiced:

"In our bag, we had a ______ that is a ______."


Write at least 5-6 sentences. If you want, you can add drawings of the items you described!
